Our Story

Crumbl Bakeria began as our dream in 2024. It all started with the smell of freshly baked bread wafting through a cozy kitchen. What began as a humble passion for kneading dough and experimenting with flavors quickly blossomed into a dream to share the joy of baking with others.

Whether it’s a morning pastry, a special occasion cake, or a batch of cookies for an afternoon pick-me-up, we’re here to make your moments sweeter, one bite at a time.

Vision

To be a beloved bakery that brings people together through delicious, handcrafted baked goods, creating memorable experiences and fostering community connections in every interaction, both in-store and online.

Mission

To create high-quality, freshly baked goods using premium organic ingredients, while fostering connections through a welcoming and delightful bakery experience.

Objectives

  • Ensure all baked goods meet the highest standards of quality and taste by sourcing local and organic ingredients.
  • Build strong relationships with our customers through exceptional service and community involvement, aiming for a customer satisfaction rate of 90% or higher.
  • Implement eco-friendly practices in our operations, including waste reduction and responsible sourcing, to minimize our environmental impact.
